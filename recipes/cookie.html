The Yummy 3000's

Description

These paleo, gluten-free, grain-free, paleo, GAPS diet friendly cookies are better than any normal chocolate chip cookies I've had! I don't even know what GAPS means

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cup blanched almond flour
  • ¼ tsp unrefined sea salt
  • ¼ tsp baking soda
  • ¼ cup maple syrup (or raw honey to make them GAPS-friendly)
  • ¼ cup melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°
  2. Lay down parchment paper on your baking sheet
  3. Take a medium bowl and mix all the dry ingredients except the chocolate chips (flour, salt, baking soda)
  4. Mix in all the wet ingredients (syrup, butter, vanilla)
  5. Finally, mix in the chocolate chips!
  6. Scoop and place on the baking sheet in tablespoons, about 3 inches apart, slightly flattening each scoop with the palm of your hand
  7. Bake 6-10 minutes. They are ready when slighlty gold-brown on the bottom. These cookies are very soft for the first 10 minutes out the oven, so don't rely on hardness to know if they are ready.
