Protein Shake

Home

Description

A shake filled with various forms of protein and nutrition!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • protein powder of choice
  • optional: milk (any kindâ€”I use oat)
  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • banana
  • honey
  • ice

Steps

  1. crack raw eggs into blender
  2. put in a scoop (or two) of protein powder
  3. pour in a bit of milk, this is more for taste because the eggs provide enough liquid for the shake to be drinkable
  4. throw in your peanut butter, a bit of honey, and ice.
  5. throw in a small banana. For a large banana, snap it in half with the peel still on. discard the peel of one half and throw the banana part in the blender. The other half can be refirgerated or frozen for next time.
  6. blend and enjoy
