Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • avocado oil spray (or olive oil, etc.)
  • 6 eggs
  • handful of spinach
  • 1 cup of halfed grape tomatoes
  • dash of basil
  • choice of seasoning

Steps

  1. spray a non-stick pan with avocado oil spray
  2. cook tomatoes 3 minutes on med-high heat, or until tender while stirring occasionally.
  3. add spinach and cook 1 minute or until wilted
  4. crack eggs into pan and let sit for a sec, then stir yoke in with whites
  5. let mixed eggs sit for a sec, then stir continuosly, adjusting heat as needed, and keeping from burning
  6. season how you like, stir
